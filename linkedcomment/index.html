Features
🔥

Trending Detection

Automatically identifies high-reach posts in your feed with color-coded badges: Trending, Hot, and Viral.

🎯

5 Comment Styles

Insightful, Supportive, Curious, Contrarian, or Criticize. Pick the tone that fits the conversation.

🧠

Personal Angle

Add your perspective — "as a startup founder" or "from a data science lens" — for authentic comments.

⚡

One-Click Insert

Generated comment drops directly into the comment or reply box. No copy-pasting needed.

🔒

Privacy First

API keys stay on your device. No external servers. No data collection. No tracking.

🌙

Dark Mode

Matches each platform's dark theme automatically. Looks great on both LinkedIn and X.com.

How It Works
1

Scroll Your Feed

LinkedComment scans posts as you scroll and badges the trending ones.

2

Click Reply

Hit the badge on any trending post to open the comment generator.

3

Pick a Style

Choose your tone and optionally add a personal angle.

4

Insert & Post

One click inserts the comment. Review, edit if you want, and post.
