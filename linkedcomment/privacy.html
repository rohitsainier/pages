LinkedComment

Privacy Policy

Effective: April 5, 2026

Overview

LinkedComment is a Chrome extension that detects trending LinkedIn posts and generates AI-powered comments. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data LinkedComment accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

LinkedComment does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or webpage content to our servers. The extension operates entirely on your local machine. We have no servers, no analytics, and no tracking.

What the Extension Accesses

  • Active Tab (LinkedIn only): LinkedComment reads post content on LinkedIn pages to extract text for comment generation. This data is processed locally and sent only to the AI provider you configure.
  • Clipboard: When you click "Copy", the generated comment is written to your clipboard. No data is sent externally.
  • Local Storage: Your settings (AI provider, model selection, thresholds, dark mode preference) are saved using Chrome's local storage. No personal information is stored.

AI Provider Communication

When you generate a comment, the post text is sent to the AI provider you choose and configure:

  • Ollama: Runs entirely on your local machine. No data leaves your device.
  • OpenAI: Post text is sent to OpenAI's API using your personal API key. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini: Post text is sent to Google's Generative AI API using your personal API key. See Google AI Terms.
  • Anthropic Claude: Post text is sent to Anthropic's API using your personal API key. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

LinkedComment does not store, log, or intercept any data sent to these providers. Communication happens directly between your browser and the provider's API.

API Keys

Your API keys are stored locally in Chrome's extension storage on your device. They are never transmitted to our servers or any third party other than the respective AI provider during API calls. We do not have access to your API keys.

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab: Access the current LinkedIn tab to read post content for comment generation.
  • clipboardWrite / clipboardRead: Copy generated comments to your clipboard.
  • storage: Save your settings (provider, model, thresholds) locally.
  • declarativeNetRequest: Modify request headers for local Ollama server communication (removes Origin header to bypass CORS restrictions on localhost only).
  • host_permissions (localhost): Communicate with local Ollama instance.
  • host_permissions (API domains): Send generation requests to OpenAI, Google Gemini, and Anthropic APIs when configured by the user.

Analytics & Tracking

LinkedComment does not include any analytics, tracking pixels, telemetry, or crash reporting. We do not track your browsing activity, extension usage, or LinkedIn engagement.

Children's Privacy

LinkedComment is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the extension evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
