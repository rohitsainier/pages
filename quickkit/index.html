Frequently asked questions

Does QuickKit require an internet connection?

No. QuickKit runs entirely on your Mac. No data is sent anywhere, ever. The only exception is the URL Shortener tool, which requires a connection to shorten links.

What macOS versions are supported?

QuickKit requires macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later. It runs natively on both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs.

Is QuickKit really free?

Yes. All 25+ tools are completely free with no paywalls, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.

Where does QuickKit live?

In your menu bar. Click the bolt icon to open the tool panel. It stays out of your Dock and out of your way.
