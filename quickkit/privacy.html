Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Overview

QuickKit is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app operates 100% offline and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

Data Collection

QuickKit does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports are sent
  • No cookies or tracking
  • No accounts or sign-ups required

Local Processing

All tools in QuickKit process data entirely on your Mac. Text you format, passwords you generate, colors you pick, and any other tool output never leaves your device.

Network Access

QuickKit requests network access for the URL Shortener tool only. No other tool requires or uses an internet connection. No data is sent to any server except the URL shortening request itself.

File Access

QuickKit may request access to files you explicitly select for tools like Image Compressor and Watermark Tool. File access is sandboxed and limited to user-selected files only.

In-App Purchases

QuickKit uses Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

QuickKit does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
